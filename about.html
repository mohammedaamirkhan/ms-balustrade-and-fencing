Who we are

MS Balustrade and Fencing

Based in Lidcombe NSW 2141, MS Balustrade and Fencing provides high-quality, durable and stylish fencing and balustrade solutions for residential and commercial properties across Sydney.

Our team specialises in custom designs, including balustrades, pool fencing, general fencing, security fencing, swing gates, double gates, sliding gates and more. Every project is handled with expert installation, superior craftsmanship and close attention to safety standards.

Contact us today for a free quote, or send your photos and rough sizes so we can advise the next step.

What we offer

  • Custom glass and metal balustrades
  • Pool fencing, boundary fencing and security fencing
  • Swing, double and sliding gate installations
  • Privacy screens for balconies, yards and side access
  • Custom designs, repairs and maintenance
  • Residential and commercial work across Sydney
