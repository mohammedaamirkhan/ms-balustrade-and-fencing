About Us

A local fabrication and installation team focused on fit, finish and safety.

From balustrades to driveway gates, we help Sydney property owners choose practical finishes that look clean and last.

Who we are

MS Balustrade and Fencing

MS Balustrade and Fencing works from Lidcombe, NSW on practical outdoor upgrades for Sydney homes and commercial sites, from clean boundary fencing to custom glass and metal details.

Our team measures carefully, explains material options clearly and installs with attention to alignment, safety and the finished look. We take pride in reliable communication from the first enquiry through to the final clean-up.

Share your plans, photos or measurements and we will advise the best next step before booking a site measure.

What we offer

  • Glass and metal balustrades
  • Pool, boundary and security fencing
  • Swing, double and sliding gate installations
  • Privacy screens for balconies, yards and side access
  • Design guidance, repairs and maintenance
  • Residential and commercial work across Sydney
